Artist Statement

Kelli Fifield identifies with art. She has trained in theater and dance. Her watercolors are detailed, realistic and delicately graceful.

Gallery History

Kelli opened her gallery in 2001 on Como Avenue in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

The gallery hosted monthly art openings featuring local and emerging artists.

The gallery has moved to three other locations in it's history, to East Hennepin Avenue, to University Avenue and finally to St. Paul on Cleveland Avenue in the historic Edith Bush Theatre Building.
